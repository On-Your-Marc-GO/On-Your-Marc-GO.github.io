About Me

Hello there! My name is Marc Arguijo. Thank you for visiting this page. Currently I am enrolled in the Houston Coding Bootcamp provided by the University of Texas at Austin. My attendance is the first step in what I hope to be a career change as a Web Developer, something I've always held interest in.

My career thus far has seen me working as an IT Professional and Customer Support Specialist across various organizations for ~6 years. I was fortunate to attend Texas A&M University and receive a degree in Management Information Systems from the Mays Business School. During my coursework there I was exposed to my first coding in other languages such as C# and Java. Ever since, I've had a tangential interest in the field and all that can be done in it.

The decision to take this change in my career is a big one for me. Much like training for and lining up at the start line of a marathon, I'm in this for the long haul and intend to see this through.
